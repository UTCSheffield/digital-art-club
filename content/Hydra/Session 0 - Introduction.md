Follow along on the website https://digital-art-club.netlify.app/

## The Aim
To create web based art on the themes of [UTC Sheffield OLP](https://www.utcsheffield.org.uk/olp/)   
- **Specialisms**
	- **Computing**
	- **Health Sciences & Social Care**
	- **Sport Science**
- **Olympic Values**
	- **Excellence**
	- **Friendship** ​
	- **Respect**
